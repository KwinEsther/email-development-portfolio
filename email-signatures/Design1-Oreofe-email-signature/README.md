---

## What’s an Email Signature?

An email signature is the block of information you see at the bottom of a professional email.  
It usually has your name, job title, phone number, maybe a logo and links.

It’s like your digital business card.

---

## Why It Matters

- Reinforces brand identity. 
- Adds credibility and professionalism.
- Makes it easier for others to contact you.
